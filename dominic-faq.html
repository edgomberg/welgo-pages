FAQ

Questions worth answering before this gets bigger.

Short answers. The point is to keep the offer clear and not blur logistics with the actual deal paths.

What is the actual offer?
Three paths: merge operations, bring listings under Welgo, or discuss a company-level path after diligence.
Is the unit walk part of the offer?
No. The unit walk with Shannon is the first proof step. It helps us see how you think about the cleaner, the owner, the review, and the next guest.
What would Dominic own locally?
Cleaners, inspections, field standards, supplies, local vendors, owner trust, small fixes, and Tucson ground truth.
What would Welgo own centrally?
Pricing, channels, guest communication, owner reporting, back office, automation, training, scorecards, and the tech layer.
What is Avvi's role?
Avvi is the operating layer behind inspections, cleaners, follow-through, and owner visibility. It should be presented as part of the system, not as proof of Dominic's economics.
Is the 10% listing structure final?
No. It is a working assumption. Ed needs to confirm it before it becomes a term.
Is the $85K buyout final?
No. Treat it as the original company-path reference. A real buyout offer needs asset, liability, contract, listing, Hostaway, furniture, IP, tax, legal, and owner-relationship diligence.
Can Dominic get Hostaway access now?
No access changes without Ed approval. If the first step goes well, Dominic can send the email he wants used. View-only access can happen only after scope is agreed.
Are the calculator numbers final?
No. They are editable planning assumptions. Do not call them Airsight. Do not call them legal terms.
What does LTV mean here?
LTV means estimated contribution profit from one listing over the years it stays with Welgo AZ. The calculator now includes PM fees, direct-booking economics, resolution revenue, upsells, scale margin, and retention.
What does CAC mean here?
CAC means the estimated cost to acquire and launch one listing. It can include owner sourcing, sales time, photos, setup, local onboarding, and early launch work.
How are direct bookings modeled?
Direct-booking mix grows with scale. The current model adds 5 percentage points of direct booking mix for every 10 listings, capped at 70%. Direct-booking revenue is gross revenue x direct mix x direct-booking fee.
What are resolutions and upsells?
Resolutions are issue resolution, restocking, local fixes, and guest or owner problems that become paid work. The working assumption is 5% of gross. Upsells are guest or owner add-ons that grow as the system gets better.
What does company value mean here?
It means estimated value of the Arizona managed-listing company from annual contribution profit, valuation multiple range, and transferable operating assets. It is not real-estate value unless Welgo actually owns the properties.
How does Dominic's ownership value work?
The calculator lets you set a potential ownership percentage. It multiplies that percentage by the estimated company value range. This is a discussion tool, not a granted equity promise.
Why is the valuation a range?
Real PM companies are usually valued from cash flow quality, not just gross revenue. Contracts, churn, owner concentration, team dependency, direct bookings, documentation, and transferability all change the multiple.

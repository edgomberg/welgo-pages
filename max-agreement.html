1. Parties

Entered into as of [date], 2026 (the "Effective Date") between Owner: Max Martin Properties LLC, an Arizona LLC, by Maxwell Martin, Manager; and Manager: Welgo Space LLC, an Arizona LLC ("Welgo" or "Manager").

2. Properties covered

Manager is appointed full-service short-term rental manager for the properties in Schedule A. The starting pool is three units on 8th Street, Tucson AZ, near Santa Rita, owner-owned and not subject to any existing Welgo lease. This Agreement does not cover, modify, or terminate the existing Santa Rita master-lease arrangement.

3. Purpose

Owner engages Manager to operate Owner's short-term rental units. Owner keeps the Properties and the upside. Manager runs the engine and earns a fee when the Properties earn revenue. If the Properties underperform during the guarantee period, Manager carries the defined guarantee risk in Section 8. This is not a lease. It gives Manager no ownership and creates no personal guaranty by Eduard Gomberg unless a separate signed document says so.

4. Term

Initial term 12 months from the Effective Date, then month-to-month unless a Party gives notice under Section 5 or the Parties sign a renewal.

5. 60-day disconnection notice

Either Party may terminate management of one or more Properties on at least 60 days' written notice. During the period Manager continues ordinary operations, prepares the Schedule F handoff package, and the Parties decide in writing what happens to future reservations. Manager may disconnect sooner for material breach, safety risk, unpaid approved expenses, missing insurance or permits, illegal STR status, platform suspension, or refusal to fund the reserve.

6. Manager scope

Marketing and revenue management; dynamic pricing; multi-platform calendar management (Airbnb, VRBO, Booking.com, Expedia, Google, direct, Hostaway); listing copy, optimization, and photography coordination; 24/7 guest communication; automated guest journey and review follow-up; housekeeping coordination, turnovers, linens, staging; light maintenance coordination; supplies and restocking; bookkeeping, payment collection, owner reporting; payout distribution; guest dispute, refund, and damage handling; STR compliance support. Manager may use employees, contractors, vendors, software, automation, and AI-assisted systems.

7. Owner responsibilities

Mortgage, taxes, insurance, HOA, utilities, internet, and ownership costs; legal authority to rent each Property as an STR; required AZ/county/city STR registrations, permits, TPT licenses, and tax accounts unless Manager agrees in writing to assist; property condition and code compliance; major capital repairs above the Schedule C threshold; ownership-side liability except Manager's gross negligence; timely access to accounts, locks, listings, portals, permits, and insurance. Owner maintains STR-appropriate insurance and names Manager as additional insured where available, with proof before reservations are accepted.

8. The guarantees (risk reversal)

Not active until Schedule D is filled and signed.

8.1 Revenue lift

Target 15% lift over the first 6 months versus the agreed baseline. Miss it, Manager refunds management fees for the shortfall per Schedule D.

8.2 Occupancy floor

Target 70% occupancy, measured per Schedule D.

8.3 72-hour launch

Listings live within 72 hours after the Schedule E access checklist is complete.

8.4 First-month money-back

Miss the revenue-lift target in the first full calendar month and Owner pays zero management fee that month.

For new units with no Welgo history, the baseline likely uses a comp-set / market benchmark, defined in Schedule D, not prior owner data.

9. Fees and Manager revenue

9.1 Management fee

18.75% of gross booking revenue per Property. "Gross booking revenue" means nightly accommodation revenue collected for completed stays. Cleaning fees, taxes, platform fees, deposits, reimbursed damages, guest service fees, upsell revenue, and pass-throughs are excluded from this base.

9.2 Guest service fee

3% charged to the guest at booking and retained by Manager. Waived on direct bookings through Welgo's channel, so direct is cheaper for the guest and builds Owner's direct base.

9.3 Procurement margin

12% on supplies and goods Manager purchases for the Properties.

9.4 Upsells

Manager builds and runs the guest-upsell program (early check-in, late checkout, mid-stay clean, pet fee, extra-guest fee, stocked items). Capture target 8% of gross booking revenue. Manager retains 100% of net upsell revenue as compensation for building and running the engine. Upsell revenue is separate from and does not reduce Owner's accommodation revenue.

9.5 Maintenance and vendor markup

15% disclosed markup on coordinated work. Manager may use an in-house crew; where used, a same-day speed guarantee applies: a qualifying same-day issue is resolved same-day or the markup on that job is waived. Qualifying-issue list in Schedule C.

9.6 Performance kicker (parked)

A performance kicker (Manager share of revenue above a comp-set baseline) is under research and is not part of this Agreement. If adopted, it is added by written amendment. Base fee for now is 18.75% only.

10. Expenses and approval authority

Manager may approve routine purchases up to $500 per unit without pre-approval. Above $500 per unit requires Owner approval, except emergencies (lock failure, water leak, HVAC failure, electrical hazard, pest, lockout, sanitation, safety, same-day turnover failure). Receipts shared in the monthly report.

11. Money handling, reserve, trust account

Guest revenue may flow through platform payout accounts, Hostaway, direct accounts, Owner accounts, Manager accounts, or a property management trust account as agreed. Owner funds an operating reserve of $500 per unit, refilled monthly from payouts, separate from the Section 10 purchase authority. Manager handles any Owner funds per Arizona law, depositing undisputed funds within three banking days. Trust-account interest, if any, belongs to Owner.

12. Monthly reporting and payouts

Owner report by the 10th for the prior month, showing gross booking revenue, cleaning collected/paid, taxes, platform fees, expenses, management fee, guest service fee, procurement margin, upsell revenue (retained by Manager), reserve balance, Owner payout, and open issues. Owner payout by the 15th after reconciliation and reserve funding.

13. Taxes, permits, compliance

Owner confirms each Property may legally operate as an STR and maintains required licenses, permits, registrations, and tax accounts. Manager may assist if agreed in writing. If a Property cannot legally operate, Manager may pause advertising or bookings, cancel affected reservations, or disconnect.

14. Listings, accounts, data, systems

Owner owns the Properties and property-specific listing assets Owner provided or paid for, subject to platform rules. Manager owns its playbooks, templates, automations, AI workflows, pricing logic, and non-property-specific systems. On termination, Manager provides a property-specific handoff package (Schedule F) but is not required to transfer proprietary systems. Platform transfer of listings or reviews is cooperated with but not guaranteed.

15. Legal relationship

Manager is an independent contractor. No partnership, joint venture, employment, franchise, lease, master lease, ownership transfer, or personal guaranty is created. Manager has no ownership interest in the Properties.

16. Licensing and broker compliance

The Parties intend compliance with Arizona property management law. Before signature, the Parties confirm whether Manager must operate through a licensed broker, designated broker, authorized licensee, or trust-account structure. If a designated-broker signature is required, this Agreement is not effective until that signature is added.

17. No assignment without owner consent

Manager may not assign to another licensee, broker, or PM company without Owner's written consent. Use of employees, contractors, vendors, and software to perform ordinary services is not an assignment.

18. Liability limits and indemnification

Manager is not liable for market changes, seasonality, platform ranking, guest conduct, neighbor or HOA complaints, pre-existing defects, Owner's permit/insurance/tax failures, platform outages or policy changes, payment holds, chargebacks, or vendor acts, except for Manager's gross negligence or willful misconduct. Owner indemnifies Manager for ownership, property, permit, tax, insurance, HOA, guest-injury, and nuisance matters except Manager's gross negligence. Manager indemnifies Owner for Manager's gross negligence, willful misconduct, or material breach.

19. Final accounting after termination

Within 5 days, a list of active guest deposit obligations. Within 35 days, reimbursement of Owner funds in Manager-controlled accounts (less unpaid obligations and disputes). Within 75 days, a final A/R and A/P list and account reconciliation.

20. Notices

By email plus one additional practical channel. Owner: maxegmartin@gmail.com. Manager: ed@welgo.space.

21. Governing law

Arizona law. Disputes in Pima County, Arizona, unless the Parties agree in writing to mediation, arbitration, or another venue.

22. Entire agreement

This Agreement and its schedules are the entire agreement for management of the Properties. Amendments must be in writing and signed by both Parties.

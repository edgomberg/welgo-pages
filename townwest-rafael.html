Three ways you get paid — in plain terms

One owner pays one management fee. We split that fee, plus maintenance, by who does what. Here's each piece, what it means, and why it exists.

1. Finder — you bring the owner

What you get: a one-time signing bonus when an owner you introduced comes on, plus a recurring slice of the management fee Welgo earns on that home, every year it stays with us.

Why it exists: origination is valuable and durable. You opened the door once; you keep earning while we keep the owner happy. It rewards you for growing the book.

The math: Welgo fee = gross × 18.5%. Your finder slice = Welgo fee × 12%. On a $29,314 home that's ~$651/yr + a $500 bonus in year one.

2. Boots — your crew runs the ground

What you get: a percent of the home's gross revenue, on homes where your crew handles the local work: turnover quality checks, owner face-time, key/access.

Why it exists: someone local has to own quality on the ground. That's real, recurring work, so it's paid as a share of what the home earns, which keeps you aligned with revenue.

The math: gross × 7%. On a $29,314 home that's ~$2,052/yr. We handle pricing, guest comms, channels, reviews. You handle local presence.

3. Maintenance — your margin on repairs

What you get: repairs and upkeep route through your vendors and crew. You charge your normal markup on that work and keep the margin.

Why it's a margin, not a flat fee: it depends on real spend. If a home needs ~$2,500 of maintenance in a year and you run it at a 15% markup, your margin is ~$375 on that home. More work, more margin. No work, no charge. It's honest because it tracks reality.

The math: annual maintenance spend × your markup %. Adjust both in the calculator below.

Bringing a new listing — what it takes

This is the part you control, and it's light. Here's exactly what happens, what we need from you, and how long it takes to go live.

  1. You introduce the owner. ~30 min
    A warm intro and a quick conversation. You're the trusted local name; we bring the numbers. We can join the call or you run it with our one-pager.
  2. We onboard the home. days 1–7
    Listing copy, photos check, pricing setup, channel connections (Airbnb + Booking + VRBO + direct), guest systems. We do this, not you.
  3. Your crew sets the ground. days 3–7
    One walkthrough, confirm the cleaning + access plan, log the turnover checklist we provide.
  4. Home goes live. ~1–2 weeks from intro
    Bookings start. Your finder and boots income begins the first month it earns.

Your time per listing: roughly 2–4 hours total, mostly the owner relationship. Welgo absorbs the heavy onboarding. Time to live: typically 1–2 weeks from the owner saying yes.

How you get paid, in plain terms

You bring owners and route their repairs. We do the short-term rental work. Here is each piece, what it means, and why it exists.

1. Finder: you bring the owner

What you get: a one-time signing bonus when an owner you introduced comes on, plus a recurring slice of the management fee Welgo earns on that home, every year it stays with us.

Why it exists: the relationship is yours and it is valuable. You opened the door once, so you keep earning while we keep the owner happy. The more owners you bring, the more it compounds.

The math: Welgo fee = gross × 18.5%. Your slice = Welgo fee × 12%. On a $29,314 home that is about $651/yr plus a $500 bonus in year one. You do zero short-term rental work to earn this.

2. Maintenance: your margin on repairs

What you get: repairs and upkeep route through your vendors. You charge your normal markup and keep the margin. This is work your shop already does.

Why it is a margin, not a flat fee: it tracks real spend. If a home needs about $2,500 of maintenance in a year at a 15% markup, that is about $375 on that home. More work, more margin. No work, no charge.

The math: annual maintenance spend × your markup %. Adjust both below.

3. Owner liaison (optional): stay the local face

What you get: if you want a hands-on role, you stay the owner's point of contact for their peace of mind and handle property access. A small flat fee per home. This is relationship work, not short-term rental operations. Welgo still runs all guest and listing work.

The math: a per-home annual fee you set. Default $600/home/yr. Skip it entirely if you would rather just refer and earn.

Bringing a home: what it takes

Your part is light, and it is the part you are already good at: the owner relationship. We do the rest.

  1. You introduce the owner. about 30 min
    A warm intro and a quick conversation. You are the trusted local name. We bring the numbers and can join the call.
  2. We onboard the home. days 1 to 7
    Listing, photos, pricing, channel connections, guest systems, cleaning crew. We do all of it, not you.
  3. We run it, you earn. ongoing
    We operate the rental around the clock. Your finder income starts the first month the home earns. Repairs route through your vendors when they come up.

Your time per home: roughly 1 to 2 hours, almost all of it the owner relationship. Short-term rental work you do: none. That is the whole point.

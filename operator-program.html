Your role

Manage the housekeeping crew (scheduling, accountability, quality). Inspect after each turnover. Handle minor repairs under $100 yourself (we cover materials). Be the first line of defense for guest + owner satisfaction.

How you're paid

Cleaning fee

Your core base

You keep the entire cleaning fee, pay your housekeepers, and keep the margin. Welgo used to keep ~20-25% for oversight. That's yours now. Pros keep 50%+.

6%

Revenue share

Of gross accommodation revenue on your units. Rewards keeping occupancy + revenue strong.

10%

NOI bonus

Of Net Operating Income above a baseline. Profitable units (controlled costs, not just revenue) earn you more.

Plus a weekly +1% green-week bonus you control: hit five-minute acks, 100% photo packs before "ready by," P1/P2 service levels, and under 1% complaints. Proof in = money on top. Paid every Friday.

Small repairs + escalation

≤$100 parts+labor: do it now, log proof, no approval. $101-250: do it, same-day note + photo + receipt. >$250: request approval; we may direct a licensed vendor and you earn 10% on the vendor invoice subtotal. No bundling — each issue is its own incident. Standard parts kit provided; consumables at actual cost.

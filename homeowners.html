We handle the Tucson rules so you don't have to

Arizona protects short-term rentals at the state level, but your TPT license, lodging tax, 24/7 contact requirement, and any HOA restrictions still have to be handled right. On Full-Service we file and remit your lodging tax and keep you compliant. Most owners do not know an HOA can quietly restrict a rental. We check before you list.

Common questions

How fast will my home go live?
Full-Service 3 to 10 days. Co-Host 72 hours from e-sign to first booking. Most companies quote 14 to 21 days.
What if revenue does not improve?
Existing listings: 15% more in six months or every fee refunded. New listings: hit 70% of market revenue by day 90 or your month-3 fee is waived.
Can I still use my own home?
Yes. Block off any dates you want on your dashboard. It is your home.
When do I get paid?
ACH on the 10th business day of each month.
Who pays cleaners and handymen?
Full-Service: Welgo collects the guest cleaning fee and pays the cleaner. Repairs under $500 pass through at cost plus 10%. Co-Host: you pay vendors direct.
Can I exit if it is not working?
Anytime with 30 days' notice. No long-term lock-in.
What if something breaks?

Full-Service: Welgo files and remits monthly. Co-Host: we give you step-by-step links and filing stays with you.

The shape

You own the properties. We become your short-term-rental operator. Pricing, listings, guests, cleaning, maintenance, reporting, tax support, all of it. You do almost nothing after you hand us access.

We earn when the units earn. If the units do well, you keep most of the gain. If we miss the targets in the first six months, we pay for the miss. You can leave on 60 days notice at any time.

What you get

  • Full-service management. Pricing, all platforms, 24/7 guest messaging, cleaning, restocking, light maintenance, reviews, and reporting.
  • More revenue than a static listing. Dynamic pricing, multi-channel distribution, and our guest-experience system.
  • Your home, protected. $1M damage and $3M liability cover on every stay. Every guest screened (ID, fraud score, party-risk). An independent inspector audits turnovers, plus a quarterly 40-point walk-through.
  • Clear downside protection. Any performance framework should be tied to work we control and capped to fee credits, not an open-ended revenue make-whole.
  • Clean monthly reporting by the 10th. Payout to you by the 15th.
  • Future direct-booking option by written addendum, after guest screening, payments, tax, insurance, damage, and calendar controls are proven.

The bonus: we get paid extra only when we beat your market

On top of the fees above, we earn a performance bonus that pays us only when your units out-earn comparable Tucson homes, never just because the market went up. We measure your revenue per available night against a fixed set of 8 to 10 comparable listings. When you beat that comp set, we share in 25% of the revenue above it. When you do not beat it, the bonus is zero. It is upside-only for us and fully aligned with you: we make more only when you make more.

The performance framework

We lead with the work we control, and any remedy should be capped to our own fees so the structure stays clear and bounded:

  • 72-hour launch. Once you give us access, your listings go live within 72 hours. Fully on us.
  • Market-beat bonus. We get paid extra only when we beat your comp set (above). Pure alignment.
  • Fee-credit remedies only. If a performance framework is included, remedies should be capped fee credits, not an open-ended revenue make-whole.
  • Comparable-set benchmark. Any benchmark should be locked in writing and should match the final signed unit scope.
  • Monthly visibility. We share revenue, expenses, management fee, cleaning remittance, and owner distribution so you see exactly where you stand.

Performance remedies, if used, assume Welgo controls pricing, calendar strategy, channel strategy, listing setup, guest communication, and reporting. Exact rules must be in the final agreement.

What you actually do

Almost nothing. You give us access once: platform or co-host access, lock access, listing info, insurance proof, permit and tax details, photos, and your payout account. After that we run it. You read a clean report each month and collect your payout.

Terms

  • 12-month agreement, then month-to-month.
  • 60-day disconnection notice, either side, any time.
  • We are an independent contractor, not a tenant. You keep full ownership. No new personal guaranty.
  • Operating reserve of $500 per unit, refilled monthly from payouts, so we handle small fixes without chasing you. Anything above $500 we clear with you first, except real emergencies.
  • If owner cancels early for reasons other than Welgo underperformance, owner reimburses documented launch costs up to the agreed cap. The cap should be locked in the final agreement before signature.

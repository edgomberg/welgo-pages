The shape

On direct bookings: when we book a guest through our own channel instead of Airbnb or VRBO, there is no platform taking a cut. We keep a 12% channel fee in place of the commission a site like VRBO would have charged you (often 15% or more). The bonus: we get paid extra only when we beat your market

On top of the fees above, we earn a performance bonus that pays us only when your units out-earn comparable Tucson homes, never just because the market went up. We measure your revenue per available night against a fixed set of 8 to 10 comparable listings. When you beat that comp set, we share in 25% of the revenue above it. When you do not beat it, the bonus is zero. It is upside-only for us and fully aligned with you: we make more only when you make more.

The guarantees, we carry the risk

We lead with the promises we control, and we cap our exposure to our own fees so a guarantee never costs you and never blows up on us:

  • 72-hour launch. Once you give us access, your listings go live within 72 hours. Fully on us.
  • Market-beat bonus. We get paid extra only when we beat your comp set (above). Pure alignment.
  • First-month money-back. Miss the revenue target in the first full month and you pay zero management fee that month.
  • Performance guarantee. For a new listing, we target 70% of comparable-market revenue by day 90, or your month-three management fee is waived. The remedy is always a credit against our own fees, capped, never cash out of our pocket beyond fees earned.
  • Occupancy target. We manage to a 70% occupancy floor as a target, measured against your market and adjusted for nights you block. We share the numbers monthly so you see exactly where you stand.

Guarantee remedies are fee-credits, capped at the fees we collected in the period, and assume you accept our dynamic pricing and keep owner-blocked nights reasonable. We define the exact rules with you on one page before any guarantee turns on.

What you actually do

Almost nothing. You give us access once: platform or co-host access, lock access, listing info, insurance proof, permit and tax details, photos, and your payout account. After that we run it. You read a clean report each month and collect your payout.

What if revenue doesn't improve?
Existing listings: +15% in six months or every fee refunded. Mirrors Houst's guarantee, but we pay cash back.
Do you guarantee results for new listings?
Yes. Hit 70% of market RevPAR or 70% occupancy by day 90, or we waive the month-3 fee.
When do I get paid?
ACH on the 10th business day each month (same cadence as GuestReady).
Who pays cleaners and handymen?
Full-Service: Welgo collects the guest cleaning fee and pays the cleaner; repairs ≤$500 pass through at cost + 10%. Co-Host: you pay vendors direct.
Can I exit if it's not working?
Anytime with 30-day notice. You only settle any unpaid Launch or Kick-Start balance.

Autohost checks ID match, biometrics, fraud score, and party risk on every booking.
What if something breaks?
Repairs under $500 fixed within 24 h; larger jobs quoted first. Receipts appear in your dashboard.
Do pets really help bookings?
Yes. Pet-friendly homes see ~27% more demand (AirDNA data). Guests pay a $45 pet fee you keep.

Full-Service: Welgo files and remits monthly. Co-Host: we give step-by-step links; filing stays with you.

Partnership FAQ · Town West × Welgo

Straight answers to the questions you'd actually ask. The short version: you bring owners, we run the short-term rentals, you earn. You do no rental operations.

The money
How exactly do I get paid?

Up to three ways, and you pick how involved you want to be. Finder: a signing bonus per owner you bring, plus a recurring slice of our management fee for as long as that home stays with us. Maintenance: repairs route through your vendors and you keep your markup. Owner liaison (optional): a small flat fee per home if you want to stay the owner's point of contact. The simplest version is just refer and earn.

Are the rates fixed?

No. The numbers are illustrative, built on our real Tucson averages. We paper exact splits once we pick the first homes. The finder slice band is roughly 10 to 15% of our fee. We land where it is fair for what you bring.

Is the finder cut really forever?

For as long as the home stays with us, yes. You bring an owner once, you earn on that home every month it is managed. That is why bringing more compounds.

The work
Do I have to run short-term rentals?

No. That is the whole point. You run zero rental operations. We do all of it: pricing, 24/7 guest support, listings, channels, cleaning crew, reviews, the night shift. You stay in your lane.

So what do I actually do?

Introduce owners who would do well on short-term rental. That is the core, and it is a warm intro plus a quick conversation, roughly an hour of your time per home. If a home needs repairs, route them through your vendors. If you want, stay the owner's local point of contact. None of that is rental operations.

What does Welgo do?

Everything that makes the home earn: dynamic pricing, 24/7 guest support, listing across Airbnb, Booking, VRBO, Expedia, and direct, the cleaning crew and turnovers, review management, owner reporting, safety escalation. That is the engine that takes a home from about 15% occupancy to about 61%.

Your owners and brand
Do my owners become Welgo's?

Not unless you want that. Default is white-label: Town West stays the brand they see, the relationship stays yours, Welgo is the invisible engine. If you would rather send owners straight to Welgo and just collect the finder income, that works too.

Does this compete with my business?

No. You do sales, long-term rentals, and owner relationships. We do short-term rental. This adds an income stream on top of what you already do, using owners you already know, without taking on a new line of work.

The risk
What if it doesn't work?

You risk nothing to find out. Pick 2 or 3 owners. We run their homes for 90 days. You see real numbers. On those first homes we guarantee a revenue floor, so if one comes in under target we cover the gap. Scale only if it works. $0 to find out.

Why is Welgo stepping back from Arizona?

We are moving from running every home ourselves to partnering with trusted local pros who bring owners. Same engine, more homes, the right local relationships out front. You are exactly the kind of partner that model is built for.

How the model works, and why
Why is finder a recurring cut, not a one-time fee?

Because the relationship is durable value. You opened the door, the home keeps earning, so you keep earning. A one-time bounty would pay you once and stop. A recurring slice means every owner you bring keeps paying you, year after year. You get a signing bonus up front too.

Why is maintenance a margin instead of a flat fee?

Because real spend varies home to home. A flat number would be made up. Tie it to actual work: a home needs some repairs in a year, you run your normal markup, you keep that margin. More work, more margin. No work, no charge.

How long to bring a home live?

Usually 1 to 2 weeks from the owner saying yes. Your part is the intro, roughly an hour. We absorb the heavy onboarding: listing, photos, pricing, channels, guest systems, cleaning crew. Then it is live and earning.

Getting started
How do we start?

Bring me 2 or 3 owners. We onboard their homes, run them 90 days, and you watch the numbers. If they prove out, we scale and paper the full partnership.

How many partners are you taking?

Five Tucson real estate pros total. You would be among the first. Early partners lock the best terms and territory.

THE FINDING THAT REFRAMES EVERYTHING

Move 1 — biggest lever

Multi-channel distribution

Your Airbnb is connected, but Booking, VRBO, Expedia, and Google are not live. We turn them all on, synced through one engine.

Impact: new demand pools fill your empty units. This is what drives occupancy from ~15% toward 55%+, and the entire revenue jump flows from it.
Move 2

Turn on Instant Book

It's off on your listings. Airbnb search actively favors instant-book, so you're buried in results you should rank for.

Impact: higher placement, more bookings, zero cost, immediate. Fewer back-and-forth requests for your team.

Close the occupancy gap

Real data: 5855 N Kolb ran 31 nights all year (~8%), 2018 E River 51 nights (~14%). Under-distributed, not mispriced.

Impact: the biggest dollar upside is doubling the empty units, not nudging the full ones. Multi-channel + dynamic pricing + 24/7 response fills them.
Move 4

Direct booking site

$616 of direct bookings all year. A real direct site cuts the platform fee on repeat guests and builds an audience you own.

Move 5

Cleaning as profit

Cleaning fees range $0-$220 across your units (one charges $0 — pure leak). We standardize and run it.

Impact: cleaning flips from a chore into ~$9-12K/yr of margin you keep, every turnover.

The monthly flow

Every month, for each unit, we run the same statement. Revenue comes in, real operating costs come out, our management fee comes out, and the rest is your payout. Same format, every month, nothing hidden.

  1. Guests book. Gross booking revenue lands (nightly rate; the cleaning fee guests pay is separate and goes to the cleaner).
  2. We subtract the real operating costs that month: platform/channel fees, cleaning, supplies and restock, utilities, and any maintenance.
  3. We subtract the management fee (a percent of revenue).
  4. What remains is your payout. We send it on a fixed date with the statement.

What is in the agreement

  • Management fee: 18.75% of nightly booking revenue.
  • Operating costs: platform fees, cleaning, supplies, utilities, maintenance, passed through at cost on the statement (no markup on utilities; supplies carry a 12% procurement margin; maintenance a 15% coordination markup).
  • Guaranteed minimum: a floor we pay you first each month, on a fixed date, before our fee. Set once we confirm your real payout. Documented for your bank.
  • True-up: each quarter we reconcile to actual and pay anything above the floor.
  • Term: 12 months, then month to month. 60 days notice either side.
  • You keep ownership. This is management, not a lease. No new personal guarantee.
